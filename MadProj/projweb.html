MAD - Medical Awesome data

1. About

MAD- Medical Awesome data is a website meant for medical advice. Middle and high school students have access to a platform where they can find sections for forums, blogs, travel and discover the world. All these help them find answers to their questions, read interesting medical articles on a variety of subjects, be protected if they attend student exchange projects and discover new things about any region around the Globe.

2. Development

2.1 Technical specifications

Languages used: PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

APIs:: to confirm that the input travel addresses exists

Databases: MediSys, Gapminder World for diseases and country information, own databases with e-mail addresses and types of users

2.2 Website structure

The website is composed of 8 pages:

  • Home
  • General topic
  • Forum
  • Blog
  • Travel adviser
  • Discover the world
  • Log in
  • About

Homepage
Homepage is the face of the website, the first interaction with the user. Here you can find aligned at the middle the title of the website, MAD. The title is an abreviation for Medical Awesome Data, a name to appeal to its targeted audience. Below the title, there is an image appropriate and suggestive for the theme of the website. After that, general information about the website are presented in the About section. This way, the users are informed about the main resources that the website provides . The meniu strip located to the left is a direct link to the pages of the website. Users can search for different information with the aid of a search box and they can navigate through the page using the scrollbar. The logo is at the top left of the page and by pressing it, the homepage can be accessed from any other page.

General Topic Page
General Topic Page is a general presentation of the main categories from the website. Here are the links of the Forum and Blog pages. The user can easily find the topic description, all questions and answers linked to the subject and all articles published by professional authors, all on one page.

Forum Page
Forum Page allows seeing questions and answers posted on the website. In the foreground there is the Best Adviser of the week to motivate users to promptly answer questions and provide quality input. The title of "Best Adviser of the Week" is awarded to the user that has the most 5 stars reviewed answers. This process resets itself every Monday at midnight to show the data from the previous week. Next to the awarded title there is the picture and name of the winner. The same system goes for "Best Question of the Week". Asking new questions is made by pressing the "New Question" button, that opens a pop-up form in which the user can fill Category and the question itself. The questions can be filtered by number of stars, popularity, most recent, oldest and category. On the upper right side there is a commercials area.

Blog Page
"Share your knowledge" is dedicated to both professional authors and users that want to get informed. Just for the first ones there is a button "New Article" that opens a pop-up that allows them to write and post their knowledge. In the left upper side there is a Topics section where users can choose to view topic specific articles. They can also discover the trending topics at that moment. To encourage users to post useful articles, the "Best Article of the Week" title is awarded to the users that submit the article with the most positive reviews. This follows the same path as the "Best Adviser of the Week" award. They are then presented in chronological order.

Travel Medical Adviser Page
The Travel Medical Adviser Page is directed both at the students that want to discover diseases and their vaccinations necessary in case of travel outside the borders and the adults that want to be informed on the matter. Discovery of this information is done by adding the starting and ending point as text in the input combo boxes named Continent, Region, Country and City. On the right side there is a suggestive image for the destination country.

Discover the World Page
Discover the World Page is designed for the students that want to discover medical information in an interactive way. They can choose a point on the Globe to learn about things like population number, population density, climate, industrial development, but most importantly, to learn about cultural factors that influence the medical environment and the common diseases. This way they can imagine a trip around the world while seeing the medical risks resulting from exposure to those areas.

The elements kept on each page are the menu, search bar, contact, media and logo sections. Navigation is facilitated by a scrollbar. By clicking the articles or questions, the user arrives to the Articles, Blog, Forum or Questions page.

2.3 Website design

Navigation The navigation is intuitive, with the menu being accessible from every page. Navigation through a page is done using a scrollbar and there is a title of each page permanently showing at the top of the page.

Visual design The visual design is a clean one. The background color is white with blue titles and green accents, simulating the hospital coats that doctors and laboratory experts wear, as an inspiration for young adults that want to pursue this domain. The main titles are in DodgerBlue. The menu is a gradient blue.
